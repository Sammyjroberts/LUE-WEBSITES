About Us
Family owned and operated, since 1990, L.U. Electric proudly serves Southern California and the greater West Coast's electrical needs. Specializing in apartments and large developments, L.U. has proven its reliability and competitive pricing. Thanks to their support and loyalty, we have been able to provide excellent service for over 2 decades.

Our mission is to provide the best service to our customers with the highest level of integrity and professionalism.

 

Our vision is to set a high standard of excellence for the electrical and security industry. We are persistently working on improving our procedures and performance in order to obtain 110% customer satisfaction.
 

Our clientele list includes large companies such as: Irvine Company, Shea Homes and Arnel Properties. Although we specialize in serving property management companies that manage single-dwelling and multi-family dwellings, we also provide service to home owners. We handle a broad spectrum of projects from replacing a 4,000 amp service to the more basic wiring of a ceiling fan.
